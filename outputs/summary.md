# ProcessLens - findings (synthetic data)

> Fictional company: **Brightway Facilities Services**. All data is synthetic and generated by `generate_data.py`.

## Headline findings

- **Quote-to-schedule drop-off:** 24% of quoted jobs never convert to a booking.
- **Bottleneck stage:** *Invoice to Payment* at a median of **14.22 days** (90th percentile 27.15 days).
- **Rework rate:** 9.8% of completed jobs are redone due to quality issues, adding delay and cost.
- **End-to-end median cycle:** 22.8 days.

## Improvement scenario

Cutting the *Invoice to Payment* stage by 40% (e.g. via automated invoice generation / templated quoting) would save ~**5.7 days** per job, reducing the end-to-end cycle to ~**17.2 days** (25% faster).
